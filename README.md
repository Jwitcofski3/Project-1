## Project Overview
This project goes through the data supplied by the UNMC Water, Climate and Health group. With the goal to identify and assess pollution hotspots in compliance with the National Ambient Air Quaility Standards and understand the affect that environmental factors have on air quality. 
## Scope of Work
### Task 1: 
Identify the top five locations with the highest mean/median PM2.5, PM10, and VOC concentrations
### Task 2:
Determine the specific dates and locations of peak pollution events 
### Task 3:
Categorize and analyze the impact of humidity andtemperature on air quality 
### Task 4:
Evaluate the correlation between geographic location and elevation on air quaility
